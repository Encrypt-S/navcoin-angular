Encrypt Your Wallet

You are about to encrypt your NavCoin StakeBox wallet. Be aware that if you lose this encryption password you will be unable to recover your coins.

Do not proceed unless you have a safe, secure back up of your password before encrypting.

Please ensure the passwords match and are at least 10 characters long

Unlock Your Wallet

You are about unlock your wallet. Be aware that this means your wallet will be able to send payments and sign messages without the use of the wallets encryption password.

We recommend only unlocking your wallet for the shortest amount of time required. Use the 'Lock Wallet' button that will display after your wallet is unlocked to lock it when you are finished.
